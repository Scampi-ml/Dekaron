Setup PayPal

Step 1

Go to https://developer.paypal.com and login with your paypal account. (Create a paypal account first if you dont have one.)

Step 2

Go to the dashboard (You may click twice to access it)

Step 3

At the page "My Apps" click the button "Create App". Give your app a name and select the sandbox developer account. To finish, click "Create App"

Step 4

At this page you can see your "Sandbox details" for testing, click "Live credentials Show" will show you the LIVE settings.

Step 5

At dekaroncms admin, if you want to go "live" change the Mode to "Live" and fill in your Client ID & Secret from the Live credentials
