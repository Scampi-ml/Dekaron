Adept

Spiritual Adept - Human - Level 9

1. Talk to the Taoist Master in Etherblade

2. Talk to the Old Chief in Hidden Heroes Village

3. Kill 9 Emaciated Footsoldiers (540 845) near the Hill of the Swarm

4. Return to the Old Chief

Spiritual Adept - Winged Elves - Level 9

1. Talk to Mangmang the Taoist in the City of the Plume

2. Talk to the Winged Elf Recluse at White Ridge

3. Kill 9 Emaciated Traitors (334 329) at White Ridge

4. Return to the Winged Elf Recluse

Spiritual Adept - Untamed - Level 9

1. Talk to Miao the Taoist in the City of the Lost

2. Talk to the Lost City Recluse south of Broken Bridge Village

3. Kill 9 Waspy Ursogs (207 527) at Dribbling Spring

4. Return to the Lost city Recluse
