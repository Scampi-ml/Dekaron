Earthguard

The Earthguard are the natural protectors of Perfect World. An ancient, subterranean race, they were selected by Pan'Gu himself to receive the gift of a "Third Eye".

The Earthguard have trained all their lives with the knowledge that, at the onset of an age of darkness and destruction, they would be called to action and depended upon. To others they may seen supernaturally calm, and at times, aloof, but this is only a byproduct of their singular motive.To guide the forces of good through the coming calamity.
