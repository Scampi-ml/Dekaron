Coalescence

Once you achieve Aware of Discord

1. Talk to the Elder of Archosaur

2. Kill Gouf, Aerox Chief in the Dragon Wilderness

3. Return to the Elder of Archosaur

4. Return to Errant Knight Fei

5. Kill Khewy, Rattus Lord south of the Valley of Disaste

6. Return to Errant Knight Fei

Aware of Discord - Level 49

7. Talk to the Celestial One at Heaven's Tear

8. Kill Fugma the Hexocelot Commander, Kashu the Hirudini Myriadept, and the Omnipotent Drake at the Fotress of the Talon

9. Return to the Celestial One

10. Talk to Lady Lo at the Village of Hidden Orchid

11. Return to the Celestial One
