Enlightened One

Once you achieve Transcendant

1. Talk to the Elder of Archosaur

2. Kill the Quillhog King east of Tusk Town

3. Return to the Elder of Archosaur

Enlightened One - Level 65

4. Talk to Tough Man Tiehi in the Village of the Dreaming Cloud

5. Investigate the Swamp of the Wraiths (546,399)

6. Return to Tough Man Tiehi

7. Talk to Elder Huo at Whetstone Keep

8. Kill the Noxious Philistine southwest of Whetstone Keep

9. Return to Elder Huo

Enlightened One - Level 69

10. Talk to Elder Huo at Whetstone Keep

11. Talk to the Celestial Elder at the City of Buddha

12. Kill Demon Hsuanling and Celestial One Chiuying at the Altar of Disbelief

13. Return to the Celestial Elder

14. Kill the Resentful Pyreostasis in the Wraithgate

15. Search the Altar of Deicide in the Wraithgate

16. Talk to the Celestial Elder at the City of Buddha

17. Open the Mysterious Chest in the Forest of Haze (676,466)

18. Talk to Hsu Hsiako in the Village of Hidden Orchid

19. Talk to Hsu Hsiako's Wife north of the Walled Stronghold

20. Return to Hsu Hsiako

21. Return to the Celestial Elder
