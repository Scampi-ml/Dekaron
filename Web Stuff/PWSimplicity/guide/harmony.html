Principle

Aware of Harmony - Level 21

1. Talk to the Mangmang the Taoist in the City of the Plume

2. Kill Carapest Terrors (287 472) east of Scorpion Valley to get the Celestial Sprite

3. Find the Hidden Celestial Sprite near the Cave of the Vicious (292 445)

4. Return to Guard Seven

5. Talk to General Summer

6. Kill 15 Heartles Wolves and 15 Treeline Wolfkin at the Mines west of Archosaur

7. Return to General Summer

8. Kill 25 Tectonic Rhinodrakes at Broken Bow Bluff

Aware of Harmony - Level 24

9. Talk to General Summer

10. Kill 15 Pyrogolth Apprentices and 15 Quillhog Enchanters at Broken Bow Bluff

11. Return to General Summer

12. Kill 15 Pyrogolth Bandits and 15 Quillhog Maguses at Broken Bow Bluff

13. Return to General Summer

14. Kill 15 Shanlang Cutlasses, 15 Dustwraith Sentinels, and Charr the Growling west of Broken Bow Bluff

15. Return to General Summer

16. Talk to the Elder of Archosaur

17. Talk to Fang the Scholar at Orchid Temple

18. Kill 15 Ossein Merchants and 15 Skeleranc Artisans at the Tomb of Heroes

19. Return to Fang the Scholar

20. Kill Tauroc Slaves northeast of Orchid Temple for the Bloodjade Grail

21. Return to Fang the Scholar

22. Kill Adalwolf Archers in the Shining Tidewood for the Core Soulgem

23. Return to Fang the Scholar

24. Kill Dustwraither Archers north of the Tomb of Heroes for the Cyan Wand

25. Return to Fang the Scholar

26. Talk to the Elder of Archosaur

27. Kill 2 Devilroot Dryads and 2 Adalwolf Sharpshooters in the Valley of the Hidden Orchid

28. Talk to the Psychic Seer at the Silver Pool

29. Kill Tauroc Sentinels southwest of the Great Lake for 15 Tauroc Pendants

30. Return to the Psychic Seer

31. Kill Torgirn Warriors east of the Stairway to Heaven for 15 Torgirn Pendants

32. Return to the Psychic Seer

33. Kill Florafang west of the Stairway to Heaven

Aware of Harmony - Level 29

34. Talk to Puhui the Taoist in Archosaur

35. Kill Longlegged Toxicons southeast of the Great Lake for 6 Spider Stingers

36. Return to Puhui the Taoist

37. Kill Farng at the Tomb of Heroes

38. Return to Puhui the Taoist

39. Talk to the Psychic Seer at the Silver Pool
