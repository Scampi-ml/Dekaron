Transcendant

Once you achieve Aware of Coalescence

1. Talk to the Elder of Archosaur

2. Talk to Talon Member Yu at Dragon's End

3. Kill the Luminoc Architect at the Fortress of the Talon

4. Return to the Elder of Archosaur

Transcendant - Level 59

5. Talk to the Celestial One at Heaven's Tear

6. Kill Zimo, Ofotis, Qianji, the Raving Drake Brute, and Gluttonix in the Valley of Disaster

7. Activate the Challenge Platform at the center of the Valley of Disaster

8. Return to the Celestial One at Heaven's Tear
