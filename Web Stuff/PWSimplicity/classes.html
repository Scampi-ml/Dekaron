Archer

As guardians of the winged elves , archers are both strong and agile.

The heightened reflexes of the Archer provide the steady aim required to vanquish enemies from afar. With an unwavering sense of pride and duty, Archers can learn to shoot arrows both elemental and poisonous in nature. Archers gain the ability to fly at level 1 providing them with an early advantage in terms of mobility. Archers are one of the primary ranged damage dealers in Perfect World, specializing in critical hit percentage and accuracy. While having somewhat of a slower start than most other classes, this class really shines above level 70.

Type:

Ranged Psychical Damage Dealer

Weapons:

Bows , Crossbows , Slingshots

Skill Tree

Blazing Arrow

Adds fire damage to regular arrows for a short while.

Frost Arrow

Slow the enemy with arrows of ice.

Vicious Arrow

Cause the enemy to suffer Wood damage over time.

Serrated Arrow

Fire a skin-ripping arrow. Causing target to bleed and take damage over time.

Sharpened Tooth Arrow

Reduces target's max hit points for a short time.

Blood Vow

Attacks the target, reducing the maximum HP of both the caster and the target. Also deals Metal damage over time to the target, amplifying the damage it takes.

Lightning Strike

A lightning bolt strikes the opponent dealing metal damage.

Thunder Shock

A great bolt of lightning inflicts Metal damage to the opponent and weakens its Metal resistance.

Thunderous Blast

Roaring thunder explodes at the target's side dealing metal damage to it and all enemies in range.

Stormrage Eagleon

A magical bird of prey dashes at your enemy.

Inflicts metal damage over time and reduces movement speed.

Winged Blessing

Increases all ranged weapon attack range.

Take Aim

Draw your bow and focus on a single shot to inflict greater Physical damage.

Quickshot

Instantly fire a powerful shot at the target. Inflicts Physical damage.

Knockback Arrow

One rapid shot inflicts Physical damage and knocks the target back several feet.

Aim Low

Focus all of your energy to aim a single shot. Paralyze target for a small amount of time.

Stunning Arrow

Fire a movement impeding shot at the opponent.

Also has a chance to stun the enemy for a short time.

Deadly Shot

Fire an intense shot causing massive damage to the enemy.

Barrage of Arrows

Use all available vigor to release countless arrows into the sky delivering devastating damage to the target and enemies in an area of effect upon impact.

Winged Shell

Focus all of your energy to instantly create a mana shield to absorb damage.

Winged Pledge

Using the power of all winged creatures to attack a melee opponent.

Wingspan

Use the divine power of the Winged to attack surrounding enemies and knock them back.

Wings of Grace

Focus all of your energy to become immune to all movement impairing effects. Also reduces all damage taken.

Bow Mastery

Increase all ranged weapon attack damage.

Flight Mastery

Increase flying speed. Only affects the Natural Elven Wings.

Wings of Protection

An improved protective spell. Can be cast on squad members.

The damage the shield absorbs depends on target's maximum HP.

Elven Alacrity

Temporarily accelerates the target to maximum speed and grants immunity to movement status ailments.

Blessing of the Condor

Summon the falcon of heaven to protect you. Greatly increases your evasion for a short time.

Awaken

Instantly recharges Spark.
