Wizzard

Trained in arcane knowledge, Wizards have developed an array of long-ranged and high-powered magic attacks utilizing the elements of nature.

As masters of the conjuring arts, Wizards are vulnerable while casting, but once the casting is complete, they deal great damage to enemies. There are two primary stat builds that players use with Wizards: Full intelligence which maximizes magical damage and high magical defense, or a light armor build which sacrifices magical damage and defense in exchange for better physical defense.

Type:

Ranged Magical Damage Dealer

Weapons:

Magic Swords, Patakas , Glaives , Wands

Skill Tree

Pyrogram

Channels the fury of the raging flame into a blazing pyrogram. Deals Fire damage.

Pyroshell

Use your energy to form a fire-resistant barrier around yourself.

Increases Fire Resistance and HP regeneration.

Crown of Flame

Place a blazing crown of fire on the opponent's head.

It burns, causing Fire damage over time.

Divine Pyrogram

Using the divine secrets of fire, a searing spell is cast on the enemy to inflict Fire damage.

The Dragon's Breath

The power of the dragon god surrounds you with a torrent of magical fire. All nearby enemies suffer constant Fire damage.

Will of the Phoenix

Summons a blazing phoenix to attack all enemies in a line and knock them back. Inflicts Fire damage.

Emberstorm

Charge up to create a violent explosion, hitting all nearby enemies at the cost of your HP. Inflicts Fire damage based on HP spent.

Blade Tempest

Focus your Chi to create dozens of flaming blades that rain down upon the target and surrounding enemies, dealing both Physical and Fire damage

Fire Mastery

Your mastery over fire increases all Fire damage.

Gush

Forces a powerful water column to spew up from the ground, dealing Water damage. Has a chance to slow the enemy.

Glacial Embrace

Uses Chi to form an icy barrier around yourself.

Increases Water Resistance and Mana regeneration.

Morning Dew

Uses Water magic to heal the target.

Frostblade

Place an icy sheen on a squad member's weapon, causing their normal attacks to inflict additional Water damage.

Glacial Snare

Launch multiple ice arrows at the enemy to trap them in an icy prison, causing Water damage. Has a chance to slow the enemy.

Black Ice Dragon Strike

Focus your Chi to summon an icy dragon that soars into the sky and smashes down onto the target and surrounding enemies, dealing Water damage. Has a chance to slow the enemies.

Water Mastery

Improves all Water magic damage.

Hailstorm

Conjure a hailstorm to rain down on the target, causing it and surrounding enemies to suffer Water damage.

Has a chance to freeze enemies.

Wellspring Quaff

Sip from the wellspring of magic to increase your maximum Mana.

Essential Sutra

Consumes Chi to recover mana, and removes the channeling time from all spells for a short time.

Manifest Virtue

Imbue your weapon with magic and Chi to increase your magic attack.

Stone Rain

A gigantic rock slams into the target, inflicting Earth damage.

Stone Barrier

Focus all of your energy to form an earthen barrier around yourself.

Increases Earth Resistance and Physical Defense.

Pitfall

Manipulate the divine force of the earth to create a sinkhole beneath the enemy, inflicting Earth damage over time and slowing the enemy. Has a chance to freeze the enemy.

Sandstorm

Launch a powerful wave of sand at target. Inflicts Earth damage and decreases accuracy.

Force of Wil

Launches an orb of force at an enemy channeling a spell, interrupting them and preventing them from casting spells.

Mountain's Seize

Focus your Chi to summon a massive boulder from a nearby mountain and slam it into your target, causing all surrounding enemies to suffer Earth damage. Has a chance to stun enemies.

Earth Mastery

Improves all Earth damage.

Elemental Shell

Manipulate the flows of magic to increase the target's Metal, Water, Fire, and Earth resistances.

Has a chance to dispel all status effects on you.

Soporific Whisper

A magical word that puts the target to sleep.

Distance Shrink

Uses the magical lines of the earth to teleport yourself forward.

Undine Strike

Strikes the target with the fury of the earth, reducing its Fire, Water, and Earth Resistances.

Elemental Invocation

A combined attack utilizing Water, Fire, and Earth magic to damage the enemy and reduce their Mana and Chi.
