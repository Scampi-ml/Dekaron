Mystic

An intelligent and versatile protector of the planet, this poised-magic user absorbs surrounding energies and unleashes them in a manner to her liking, whether it be a warm, healing glow, or a vicious kinetic bolt

If the Seeker is the backbone of the Earthguard army, then the Mystic is the bundle of muscle and nerves that keeps it protected and flexible. Proficient in several fields of magic, Mystics are comfortable serving many roles in and around the battlefield from dealing direct ranged damage, to providing tactical heals, to dishing out powerful status effects.

Type:

Ranged Magic Damage Dealer, Support

Weapons:

Wands, Patakas

Skill Tree

Nature's Vengeance

Razor-sharp thorns paint your target green and red.

Deals high Wood damage to the target and reduces its Speed.

Also causes a special status, under which Absorb Soul deals double damage.

Swirling Mist

Mists pour in, casting eerie shadows that attack the target.

If the target's Health is lower than 60%, it takes Wood damage for 9 seconds.

Absorb Soul

Calling upon the earth, you concentrate all your power and deal large amounts of Physical damage to the target.

Gale Force

A whirlwind whips around you.

Deals large amounts of Wood damage to enemies around you.

Also has a chance to Stun or Freeze the target.

Bramble Tornado

The wind whips up brambles and thorns to attack your enemies.

Deals a large amount of Wood damage. Knocks target back 12 meters.

Thicket

Summons giant plants to attack for you.

Deals large amounts of damage to the target and has a chance to Stun or Silence them.

Break in the Clouds

An invigorating sunbeam illuminates the target.

Recovers Health.

Falling Petals

Fuses the target with a magical aura.

Heals the target when it takes damage.

Resurrect

Prevents the soul of the dead from escaping, and resurrects the target.

Comforting Mist

The land breathes life into all its children.

Heals yourself and all friendly targets around you.

Devil Chihyu

Summons a furious demon to aid you in combat with its Physical attacks.

Storm Mistress

Summons a fierce avatar to aid you in combat.

Uses Metal skills.

Salvation Emerges

Summons a guardian with high Defense to aid you in combat.

Cragglord

Summons a flaming demon to the battlefield.

The summoned demon attacks with powerful Fire skills.

Heal Summon

Heals your summoned follower.

Transference

Transfers part of your Mana to your summon.

Energy Leech

Sacrifice your current summon to give yourself a boost.

Punishing Sting

Summons a Stinging Nettle to attack the enemy.

Attacks a single target.

Befuddling Creeper

Summons a wicked vine that performs AOE curses.

Spidervine

Summons a tangling thread that causes AOE Slow.

Listless Blossom

Summons a plant that causes an AOE Sleep effect.

Healing Herb

Summons an herb that performs AOE healing.

Vital Herb

Summons an herb that performs powerful AOE healing.

Verdant Shell

A devout follower can receive blessings from the natural world.

Increases Wood and Water resistances and Physical Defense.

Lysing

Obliterates your summoned plants, dealing damage to nearby targets and causing strange effects.

Wood Mastery

Increase all Wood damage.

Rapid Growth

You can feel the spirit of nature flowing within you.

Reduces Channeling time by 20% and increases Attack.
