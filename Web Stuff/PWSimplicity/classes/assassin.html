Asssassin

Assassins are skillful melee combatants, though much faster than Blademasters or Barbarians.

Daggers are their weapons of choice. In Critical Hit form, they can deal more damage than other classes in most circumstances. With their vital ability to stealth in addition to their damage, Assassins are a terrifying addition to any battlefield.

Type:

Melee Psychical Damage Dealer, Stealth

Weapons:

Daggers

Skill Tree

Subsea Strike

An advanced assassin technique.

Deals Physical damage to all targets within 8 meters.

Causes an increasing damage effect.

Dagger Devotion

Increases all damage with daggers.

Twin Strike

A swift strike with both daggers.

Deals a great amount of Physical damage.

Slipstream Strike

A superb assassin skill.

Deals a great amount of Physical damage to the target.

Raving Slash

Deals Physical damage to the target and slows its movement speed.

Deep Sting

A bloody assassin skill.

Deals a great amount of Physical damage and has a chance to put the target to sleep.

Rib Strike

Deals Physical damage to the target, and reduces its attack speed.

Throatcut

A swift throat cut.

Deals high damage and has a high chance to silence the target.

Earthen Rift

Detonate the ground under the enemy.

Deals great Physical damage to all enemy units around you.

Headhunt

A swift blow to the head.

Deals Physical damage and stuns the target.

Sharp Observer

Increases your Awareness level.

Shadow Escape

Force yourself into stealth.

Dispel all negative status.

Enemies with an Awareness Level lower than your Stealth Level can not see you.

Increases own Chi.

Power Dash

Dash into the enemy formation with a death determination.

Deals Physical damage and increases own Critstrike rate.

Cat-Like Tread

Increases your Stealth level.

Shadow Walk

The Assassins can hide themselves from enemies.

Enemies with an Awareness Level lower than your Stealth Level can't see you.

Only usable outside of combat.

Focused Mind

Temporarily increases your chance to avoid enemy skills.

Bloodpaint

Allows friendly units within range to recover some HP each time they use a Physical attack.

This skill only works on Blademasters, Barbarians, and Assassins.

Deaden Nerves

The secret technique of Tideborn Assassins.

Avoid one attack that would otherwise kill you.

Recovers some HP upon avoiding death.

Tidal Protection

Temporarily increases your chance to avoid negative status effects.

Healing Trance

Weaves an aura of healing around you, and heals you quickly.

Condensed Thorn

Condenses cold water into a thorn of frost. Fortifies yourself while hurting the enemy.

Puncture Wound

Deals a great amount of damage and causes a bleed effect.

Wolf Emblem

Temporarily increases your critical strike damage.

Knife Throw

Throw your daggers toward the target.

Deals a great amount of damage and has a chance to interrupt channeling.

Tackling Slash

A tackling skill for Assassins.

Deals Physical damage and immobilizes the target.

Increases own Chi.

Chill of the Deep

Focus on the chill of the ocean depths.

Reduces attack speed but increases attack level.

Rising Dragon Strike

An advanced Assassin technique.

Deals a great amount of damage to the target, and increases your Chi.

Perfect accuracy.

Inner Harmony

Greatly increases your own Chi.

Spell Cutter

Interrupts enemy channeling with a flashing attack.

Tide Form

Transform into a fish.

Increases Defense Level and Swimming Speed.

Only available in water.

Windpush

Increases own movement speed.

Shadow Jump

Instantly move to the target's location.

Maze Steps

A special movement of Tideborn Assassins.

Increase movement speed, and become immune to stuns and immobilization.

Shadow Teleport

The Assassin can focus on the ground and swiftly dash to the target.

Instantly move to the target's location, and Stun the target.

Blood Frenzy

When an Assassin is in a critical situation, they can rampage and enter this Blood Frenzy status.
