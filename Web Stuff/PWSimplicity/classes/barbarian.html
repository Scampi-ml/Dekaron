Barbarian

Barbarians have evolved from pandas, lions, and other beasts.

The Barbarian is a natural-born warrior on the battlefield, and is comfortable taking hits and returning the punishment. These fighters are also capable of shape-shifting into white tiger form in order to maximize their physical defense and damage mitigation. Barbarians can allocate their stats a variety of ways, either building for maximum defense or maximum damage.

Type:

Tank, Melee Psychical Damage Dealer

Weapons:

Dual Swords, Dual Blades, Blades, Swords, Scythes, Spears, Clubs, Poleaxes, Sledgehammers, Dual Hand Axe, Dual Hammers, Fist Weapons

Skill Tree

Stomp of the Beast King

Execute a concussive bash. The enemy suffers Physical damage.

Garrotte

Wrench the opponent inflicting Physical damage and causing it to bleed.

Mighty Swing

Take a mighty swing dealing massive damage to a single enemy. Has a chance to stun.

Penetrate Armor

Smash the opponent with serious force, inflicting Physical damage and rendering the opponent's Physical defenses reduced.

Bestial Onslaught

Reduces enemies' chance to dodge your attacks.

Violent Triumph

Work the caster into a frenzy that will to possess the body redering the caster immune to stun, freeze and other status ailments.

Axe and Hammer Mastery

Increases all axe and mace weapon damage.

Swell

A sideswiping thrust of your weapon forms a jet stream of air inflicting Physical damage to enemies within a fanlike radius in front of you.

Firestorm

Emit a scorching blaze of light dealing Physical damage to enemies in a straight line in front of you. They suffer Fire damage over time.

Slam

Leap up and stomp the ground. Creates a massive shockwave inflicting damage to all nearby enemies knocking them back.

Armageddon

Use every ounce of energy to consume half of your health and mana points and convert it into a large area effect attack.

Ancestral Rage

Attack the target in a range of 18 meters, dealing great damage and freezing the target for 8 seconds.

Swimming Mastery

Has no effect.

Feral Regeneration

Increase your health regeneration rate.

Beast King's Inspiration

A great growl grants oneself and nearby party members an increase in max hit points.

Blood Bath

Reduces max hit points to increase chance to hit.

Strength of the Titans

A raging roar renders you and party members more powerful, increasing Physical damage.

Clean Sweep

Deals base damage to target. Has a 20% chance to remove target's positive buffs.

True Form

Morph into a white tiger to increase maximum hit points and movement speed but reduce attack damage.

Flesh Ream

Rip the enemy's flesh, drawing their aggression onto you.

Also causes them to suffer bleeding damage over time.

Alacrity of the Beast

Pounce onto the enemy canceling all spell channeling as well as inflicting Physical damage.

Devour

Snap at the opponent, dealing Physical damage and reducing their Physical Defense.

Surf Impact

Pounce on the target with devastating force, causing it and surrounding enemies to suffer Physical damage and reducing their movement speed.

Sunder

The full force of one's vigor is unleashed with this swirling blow.

Deals Physical damage and causes enemies to bleed over time.

This attack also leeches life from the target.

Shapeshifting Intensity

Increases Physical Defense while in Tiger Form.

Bestial Rage

When active, vigor increases with damage taken.

Poison Fang

All normal attacks deal additional Wood damage as well.

Roar

With a raging roar, all nearby enemies are forced to attack you.

Frighten

Strike fear into the hearts of nearby enemies, reducing their speed and attack damage.

Untamed Wrath

A tremendous roar that causes great hatred and temporarily stuns all nearby targets.

Invoke the Spirit

The spirit of the Black Warrior Tortoise possesses you. All damage taken will be reduced but your movement speed is also reduced.
