Psychic

Psychics are masters of mental magic and are capable of casting both strong offensive and defensive spells.

They no longer pursue mastery of the elements, but instead manipulate the essence of the instinct of creatures. They manifest their will through Soulforce to enhance themselves and their allies while harming their enemies.

Type:

Ranged Magic Damage Dealer, Support

Weapons:

Soulsphere

Skill Tree

Aqua Impact

Concentrate your magic powers and discharge them towards the target in one shot.

Deals Water damage and has a chance to slow the target.

Black Voodoo

Increase own Attack Level.

Reduce own Defense Level.

Torrent

Condenses the Water element and shoots it against the target.

Deals constant Water damage.

Aqua Cannon

Creates a ball of Water to strike the enemy.

Deals Water damage and has a chance to slow nearby targets.

Glacial Shards

Shoot a burst of Glacial Shards that deals Water damage to all enemy units within range.

Has a chance to stun the targets.

Disturb Soul

Disturb the target. Increases spell channeling time.

Tide Spirit

Gains the power of the Water element.

Increases own Magical Attack.

Increases channeling speed of all skills.

This skill does not stack with the Rainbow Blessing genie skill.

Aqua Spirit

You are highly attuned to Water magic, increasing all Water damage.

Bubble of Life

Constantly recover HP of all nearby friendly targets.

Empowered Vigor

Increases the target's power of recovery.

Increases the healing effect of HP potions and skills.

Reduces the cooldown time of Guardian Charms.

Red Tide

Deals Water damage to the target and all enemy units around them.

Has a chance to cause bleeding.

Earthen Spirit

Your connection to the earth increases all Earth damage.

Earth Vector

Summon the element of Earth to attack enemies.

Deals Earth Damage to the target and all enemy units around them.

Has a chance to stun enemies.

Tide Form

Transform into a fish.

Increases Defense Level and Swimming Speed.

Only available in water.

Spirit Blast

Uphold your weapon with the power of Earth element.

Deals Earth damage.

White Voodoo

Increase own Defense Level.

Reduce own Attack Level.

Landslide

Detonates the energy within the earth.

Deals Earth damage, interrupts spell channeling, and knocks back the target.

The knockback effect won't work on players.

Sand Trap

Creates a force of elemental Earth to strike the target.

Deals constant Earth damage.

Sandburst Blast

Creates a sand storm and deals Earth damage to the target and all enemies within range.

Has a chance to lower their Accuracy.

Soulburn

Interferes with the target's magic power and damages the target's psyche.

Each time the target attacks, it takes damage equal to your Soulforce.

Psychic Will

Summon the element of Earth to attack enemies.

Deals Earth Damage to the target and all enemy units around them.

Has a chance to stun enemies.

Crystal Light

Crystallize cold water vapor and shoot a chilled bolt at your targets.

Diminished Vigor

Reduces target's power of recovery.

Lowers the effect of HP recovering potions and skills.

Increases the cooldown time of Guardian Charms.

Essential Sutra

Consumes Chi to recover mana, and removes the channeling time from all spells for a short time.

Sandball Clash

Project a powerful wave of sand onto a target. Inflicts Earth damage.

Soul of Vengeance

The target automatically deals Physical damage to attackers.

The damage is equal to 8% of your Soulforce.

Soul of Stunning

Self cast only.

Stun your attacker upon being hit.

Your Soulforce determines the stun duration.

Soul of Silence

The target automatically silences attackers.

The success rate is based on your Soulforce.

Soul of Retaliation

Reflects negative status effects and damage onto your attacker.

Your Soulforce determines the success rate.

Stone Smasher

Use all of one's energy to invoke the spirit of the Mountain.

Slams the target and all enemies around it.
