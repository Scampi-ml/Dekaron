Cleric

As the healers of Perfect World, Clerics are masters of supportive and recovery spells, which makes them invaluable addition to any group or party.

In addition, Clerics specialize in using Metal elemental attacks, and can curse their enemies to reduce their defense, speed, or attack damage. As damage dealers, Clerics are incredibly flexible and versatile. They posses both physical and magical ranged attacks allowing them to take down any target regardless of what type of armor they may be wearing. Clerics are traditionally built with low physical defense, but can also be adjusted statistically to wear light armor and as a result become more durable in the face of physical damage.

Type:

Healer, Support

Weapons:

Wands, Magic Swords, Patakas, Glaives

Skill Tree

Blessing of the Purehearted

An Elven blessing that restores HP.

Ironheart Blessing

Bless the target to restore HP over time.

Wellspring Surge

Rapidly heals the target with a surge of magic.

Revive

Revive a squad member. Decreases experience loss from death.

Purify

Remove all negative effect on one friendly target.

Chromatic Healing Beam

Heals a large amount of health for surrounding party members.

Stream of Rejuvenation

Instantly heal a large amount of target's hit points.

Also adds a heal over time to that target.

Regeneration Aura

Conjure an array of hit point recovery and damage reduction that constantly consumes your mana points.

Effects all party members.

Vanguard Spirit

Grant the target additional Physical Defense.

Greater Protective Aura

Grant all party members within range an increase to Physical Defense.

Guardian Light

Temporarily reduces damage taken and increases movement speed.

Magic Shell

Grants the target additional Magic Defense.

Aegis Spirit

Grant all party members within range an increase in Magic Defense.

Wings of Protection

An improved protective spell. Can be cast on squad members.

The damage the shield absorbs depends on target's maximum HP.

Heaven's Wrath

Use all of your vigor to form a mystical array.

By consuming mana points Physical and Magic attack damage are both increased.

Flight Mastery

Increase flying speed. Only affects the Natural Elven Wings.

Celestial Guardian's Seal

Increase the target's HP and Mana regeneration.

Exalted Renewal

Increase the Health and Mana regeneration rate for all nearby party members.

Spirit's Gift

Increase the target's magic attack.

Arcane Empowerment

Grant all party members in range an increase to magical damage.

Seal of the God

Seals and freezes the target, making it receive less damage from all attacks.

Plume Shot

Focus your psychic energy to form a magical arrow to attack the enemy. Inflicts Physical damage.

Plume Shell

Convert your Mana and Chi into a protective barrier that absorbs incoming Physical damage.

Razor Feathers

Draw power from your wings to fling dozens of magical quills at your target and all nearby enemies. Inflicts Physical damage.

Elemental Seal

Manipulate the magic in the air to form an Elemental Seal.

Reduces your Magic Defense to reduce the enemy's Magic Defense.

Dimensional Seal

Manipulate the magic in your armor to form a Dimensional Seal.

Reduces your Physical Defense to reduce the enemy's Physical Defense.

Silent Seal

Manipulate your energy to form the Seal of Silence.

Reduce one's own magic attack damage to paralyze the enemy.

Chromatic Seal

Manipulate your inner-energy to form the Seal of Colors.

Reduces your movement speed to put an enemy to sleep.

Elven Boon

Attacks the target with the spirit of wind. Has a chance to temporarily seal the target.

Also has a chance to heal the caster.

Great Cyclone

Summon an immense cyclone around the enemy, inflicting Metal damage. Has a chance to reduce the enemy's movement speed.

Thunderball

Create a sphere of lightning over the enemy's head, dealing Metal damage and causing the enemy to suffer additional Metal damage over time.

Siren's Kiss

Conjure a sweeping breeze inflicting Metal damage to all nearby enemies. Enemies are also paralyzed for a short time.

Wield Thunder

Call upon the power of the heavens to cast a chain of lightning at an enemy, dealing massive Metal damage to a single target.

Tempest

Focus all of your energy to cast down countless thunder bolts causing target and the enemies around it to suffer Metal damage.

Also has a chance to reduce their movement speed.

Metal Mastery

Improves all Metal magic damage.
