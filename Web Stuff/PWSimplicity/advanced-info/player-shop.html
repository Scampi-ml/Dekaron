Player Shop

Within the game users have the option of opening their own character shop for other users to access. These shops have the ability to both sell and buy from other users. To access this feature simply click on the Consignment Shop within your Emotes page under the Trade category.

Player Shop

1. Click to open Shop Menu

2. Place the item you want to sell here.

3. Place the item you want to purchase here.

4. Name of Shop.

5. Maximum of coins you are willing to spend on buying items.

6. Resets window.

7. Confirm and open shop.

8. Cancel and closes shop.
