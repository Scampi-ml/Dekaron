Player Storage

You may store extra items you have at the bank. These banks can be accessed through the banker NPCs. When you speak to a banker, you will be able to perform several actions.

Safe service - You can access your safety box and deposit/withdraw items and/or coins.

Change safe code - You can change the code used to access your safety box here.

Quest - The banker offers two quests which will allow you to increase your safety box and inventory size upon completion of the quests.

Player Bank Storage

This is the safety deposit box interface.

1. Amount of stored coins. Click on the coin icon in your inventory to deposit coins. Click on the coin icon in the safety box to withdraw stored coins.

2. Item slots. Drag and drop items to and from your inventory to the item slots in the safety box to deposit/withdraw items. The number of item slots available may increase on completion of the banker quests.

3. Withdraw gold from your bank to your inventory.

Bank Extension

First part of the quest will allow you to extend your current bank to 24 slots. It requires 5 of each the following:

  • Mystical Meat

  • Disguise Skin

  • Elemental Dust

  • Soft Fur

  • Antenna

  • Rhino Horn

First part of the quest will allow you to extend your current bank to 32 slots. It requires 10 of each the following:

  • Mystical Claw

  • Complete Sack

  • Elemental Fragment

  • Honey Juice

  • Dev of Herbs

  • Dull Claw

Boutique - Storage Extension

Inventory Extension Stone

Take it the banker and he will extend your inventory's capacity.

Safe Extension Stone

Take it the banker and he will extend your safe's capacity.

Super Inventory Extension

Like a normal inventory stone... only SUPER! Use it at the Inventory Master to extend your safe to the maxmimum capacity of 64.

Super Safe Extension

Like a normal safe stone... only SUPER! Use it at the Inventory Master to extend your safe to the maxmimum capacity of 80.
