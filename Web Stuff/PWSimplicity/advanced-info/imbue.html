Imbue

Weapons and armors may come with sockets, which can be imbued with gems. Imbuing a weapon/armor would bring great benefits to your character. Different Gems is empowered with different attributes.

For example it can raise your physical attack, magicial attack, physical defense, magic defense, accuracy, evasion, etc. imbuing your weapon can also bring unique glow effects to your weapon/armor. To refine your Gems to a more higher level you can visit the Jewels craftsman located in every town.

Below is a list of gems that can be crafted, dropped from monsters, rewarded from quests or 'Lucky items'.

Each weapon can be equipped with 2 sockets and armor can be equipped with 4 sockets. Different level or type of weapon or armor may require more socket stones.

Below here are the steps and item needed to socket your weapon/armor.

Boutique - Socket

Socket Stone

A stone being collected by PW Boutique Agents. Lets you add sockets to gears.
