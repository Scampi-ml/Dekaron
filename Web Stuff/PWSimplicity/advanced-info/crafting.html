Crafting

In Perfect World International weapons, armor, and accessories can be created by the players. These manufacturing skills or professions are called Apothecary, Blacksmith, Armorsmith, and Craftsman.

Upon reaching level 5 these trade skills become available. Simply speak witd the Elder of your city and he will assist you.

Manufacturing Skills

Blacksmith:

Able to manufacture all kinds of weapons including blades, swords, axes, hammers, bows, and more.

Tailoring:

Able to manufacture all kinds of armor and defensive equipment.

Jeweler:

Able to manufacture all kinds of accessories including necklaces, belts and rings.

Apothecary:

Able to manufacture all kinds of remedies and charms.

Manufacturing

To advance in your manufacturing skills, please visit tde corresponding NPC of that profession. As you advance in rank you will need to complete additional quests or aquire books.

As you level up your trade skill you will need to create items of your grade. So if you are level 2 you will need to craft level 2 items in order to advance.

Higher level skill grades require that you attain a certain level before advancing to them.
