Winged Elves

The Winged Elves hail from an ancient and divine bloodline. Their exquisite beauty and naturally-developing wings are the result of divine seed borne by mortal maidens.

This provides them with heightened sense, as well as the ability to fly. In Combat, Winged Elves specialize in archery and supportive healing magic.

Start location:

Battlemark Village

Home City:

Plume City

Racial Mount:

Wings

Available Classes

Archer | Cleric
