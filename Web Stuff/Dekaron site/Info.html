Dekaron Crisis — The Game

 

General

Dekaron Crisis is a free to play onlinerole-playing game with an emphasis on PVP and PVE. We bring to you a truly unique server that provides to you the best Dekaron expeirence.

What does Dekaron Crisis offer?

Advanced EXP System – You start as a young lone warrior, but soon get the chance to band together with other to form an alliance. The advanced EXP system provides user expierence and as your warrior learns the world, he advances in strength and other attributes.

Argating/Refining System – All Weapons and Armor have the ability to be refined. The max level of refine is +9. The higher the level of the item, the more powerful it becomes. You can use the three types of Argates to refine your items. Copper Argates for level +1~3, Silver Argates for +4~6, and Gold Argates for +7~9. Below are the argating rates for each level. You can use a Blacksmith's Talisman (available in the D-Shop) to enhance you rrates.

+1 = 99% | +2 = 90% | +3 = 85% | +4 = 70% | +5 = 50% | +6 = 45% | +7 = 30% | +8 = 20% | +9 = 12%

PvP – Be it duels, spontaneous skirmishes, regularly-held events and battlegrounds or an all-out declaration of war on other guilds… Dekaron Evolution has everything a PvP fan could wish for! Your efforts will also not go unnoticed: Look for your name at the top of the ranking lists.

Flexibility and depth - Dekaron Crisis doesn’t just offer a wealth of features, but also has a depth never seen before in a game of this type. The detailed and elaborate game elements form a complex and multi-faceted world that - unlike in most other RPGs - is not static but rather can be changed by the player’s choices.

What do I need to do to be able to play Dekaron Crisis?

Just create an account, and download the client. It's really that simple. Once the client is downloaded, the installer will guide you through the rest of the way.

How much does Dekaron Crisis cost?

Dekaron Crisis is completely free-to-play! You can play the game in its entirety without having to purchase the game or pay a monthly subscription fee. You do have the option to purchase premium items. You can obtain coins by donating to the server.
